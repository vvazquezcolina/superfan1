Hotels downtown KC or along I-70 corridor. BBQ tradition in stadium lots legendary. Sources: FIFA.com, arrowheadstadium.com, chiefs.com

    - **Gillette Stadium** (Boston): Opened 2002. 65,878 capacity. Home of New England Patriots and Revolution. Located in Foxborough, MA -- 50km southwest of Boston (furthest stadium from its "host city" in WC2026). NO regular transit -- special event MBTA commuter rail or drive. Patriot Place shopping/dining complex adjacent. Hotels limited in Foxborough, most fans stay in Providence or Boston. Honest about transit challenge. Sources: FIFA.com, gillettestadium.com

July weather 25-30C, occasionally humid. Hotels in Entertainment District, King West, or Liberty Village. eTA required for most visitors. Sources: FIFA.com, bmofield.com, torontofc.ca

    - **BC Place** (Vancouver): Opened 1983, fully renovated 2011 with retractable roof. 54,500 capacity. Home of Vancouver Whitecaps (MLS). Downtown Vancouver, False Creek area. SkyTrain (Stadium-Chinatown station) directly adjacent -- most centrally-located WC2026 stadium. Retractable roof handles Vancouver rain. Surrounded by mountains and Pacific Ocean. Hotels in Yaletown, Gastown, or West End. eTA required for most visitors. Sources: FIFA.com, bcplace.com, whitecapsfc.com
